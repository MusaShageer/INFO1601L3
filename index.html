Sample Poem

If Our Demons Cannot Dance

I will not have you without the darkness that hides within you.
I will not let you have me without the madness that makes me.
If our demons cannot dance, neither can we.

About Nikita Gill

Nikita Gill is a British-Indian poet, writer, and illustrator known for her modern and deeply emotional poetry. Born in India, she later moved to the UK, where she gained recognition for her feminist themes, reflections on love, mental health, and mythology.
